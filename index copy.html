FAQ's

  • Plug & Play
  • Qi Standard
  • Speed

1. Can I charge multiple devices at the same time on one Hitz wireless charger?

Hitz wireless charger support the Qi standard from Wireless Power consortium (WPC). According to Qi specification only one device/receiver can be charged on one Qi compatible transmitter coil at any given time.

2. Is it possible to charge tablets on Hitz wireless charging accessories?

At the moment, Hitz transmitters deliver up to 5W of output power, which means that it is not enough output power on all tablets – and they may charge only slowly on Hitz transmitters. However, some tablets, like Google Nexus 7 and Apple iPad mini charge at a normal pace on Hitz transmitters.

3. How quickly will my device charge?

Wireless charging on Hitz transmitters is as fast as charging with normal wall charger. However, please note that some wireless charging receivers may charge at a slightly slower pace

4. My phone feels warm to touch after charging, should I be concerned?

It’s normal that your phone gets warm when charging wirelessly. However, this is not a concern. All Hitz wireless chargers have gone through appropriate Health and Safety measures. If you charge your phone with a normal wall charger, you can feel that the charger gets hot. It’s the same with wireless charging.

5. Are Hitz wireless chargers water tight?

Yes, Hitz wireless chargers are fully splash proof.

6. What do I do if my device returns “accessory not supported” when placed on a Hitz wireless charging pad?

Try to reposition the device on the charger. If notification persists, a hard reboot may be needed.

7. If my wireless charger emits a continual beep what does that mean?

Your device is misaligned, please remove your handset and center back onto the charger.

8. How do I know if my device is not charging properly?

No Audio Beep / The charging bar on the phone is NOT indicating charging.

9. If my device is listed as Qi Compatible, what do I need to do to get it to charge wirelessly?

If your device appears under the Qi Compatible list then you have the technology built into your device, it just needs to be activated by placing it onto a Hitz charger.
